FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

🐕 Do you offer pet grooming for all breeds?

Yes! Our trained groomers handle all dog and cat breeds. We provide breed-specific styling and can accommodate anxious or first-time pets with gentle handling techniques.

📦 Do you deliver pet food?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery from our Sector 11 location across Panchkula and the Tricity area. Simply call or WhatsApp your order to +91 78144 44421.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking. For deliveries, prepaid UPI is preferred.

🐱 Do you sell pets?

Pet availability varies. Please call our Sector 11 store at +91 78144 44421 to check current availability. We can also help connect you with reputable breeders and adoption centers.

📅 Do I need to book grooming in advance?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking ahead (especially on weekends) to ensure your preferred time slot. Call or WhatsApp to book.

🏆 What brands of pet food do you carry?

We stock all major brands including Royal Canin, Pedigree, Drools, Farmina, Orijen, Acana, Whiskas, Me-O, Sheba, Arden Grange, and many more. If we don't have it, we can order it for you!
